ABOUT US

Olute Football Club was formed in January 2021 to discover young soccer talents in Nigeria and the rest of Africa and help them become better individuals in society. We believe in the vast human and natural resources Nigeria and Africa possess, especially in the football industry. Olute FC was created to recognize, develop, and deploy these talents.

OUR MISSION

Our aim is to produce the next generation of big stars and talents in the world of football, and give them the best education in the game that will benefit them for life.

OUR STORY

Olute Football Association was founded in 2021 by Audu Boma Aliu, a native of Kogi State. Driven by his passion for football, Boma not only focuses on developing players but also on uplifting the wider community—a principle that has defined Olute FA since its inception.

Audu Boma Aliu

A CLUB FOR THE COMMUNITY

Olute Football Association aims to be more than just a football club. Many young, talented players lack the support to pursue the game. We strive to provide them with necessities, football kits, proper training facilities, and community support. We are committed to youth development and supporting local businesses.

Developing football talent and character since 2021. Olute FC is committed to excellence in youth sports development.
